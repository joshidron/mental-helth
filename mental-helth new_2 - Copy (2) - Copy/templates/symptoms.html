Prajna Path Assessment

Emotional Well-being

Questions related to your mood, anxiety, and emotional stability.

Do you often worry too much about everyday things, feel restless, or find it hard to relax?
Do you feel sad, hopeless, or have you lost interest in things you used to enjoy?

Behavioral Patterns

Questions about habits, compulsions, and attention.

Do you have thoughts that keep coming back (obsessions) or feel the need to do things over and over (compulsions)?
Do you find it hard to focus, stay still, or control your impulses?
Do you worry a lot about your body image, weight, or eating habits?

Personality

Questions about relationships, identity, and past trauma.

Do your relationships feel unstable, or do you act impulsively without thinking?
Do you have flashbacks, bad dreams, or anxiety about a scary event from the past?

Minor Case

Common daily life stressors.

Do you feel very tired, easily annoyed, or burned out from work or daily life?

Awareness & Highlights

Understanding neurodiversity.

Do you find it hard to understand social cues, make eye contact, or communicate with others?
